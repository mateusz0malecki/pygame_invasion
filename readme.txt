In game Invasion player is controlling a spaceship displayed at the bottom of the screen. Player can move the 
spaceship to the left and to the right by pressing arrow keys and shoot bullets by pressing space. After begging of 
the game fleet of bad guys will appear on the screen. Player's task is to shoot them all. While one fleet is finished, 
another is about to come and move faster to destroy the spaceship. If any of the bad guys touches the spaceship or 
bottom of the screen one ship is lost. After loosing 3 ships game is over. Try to get as many points as you can!
